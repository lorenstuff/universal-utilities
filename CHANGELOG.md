* NumberLib
	* `clampNumber` -> `clamp`

	* `roundNumberDownToNearest` -> `roundDownToNearest`

	* `roundNumberUpToNearest` -> `roundUpToNearest`

	* `escapeSqlLikeString` -> `escapeLikeString`
* StringLib
	* `padStringWithNull` -> `padNull`
